Ensuring Equal Access to Hockey

Hockey is not for everyone, but it’s time to change the narrative. The Anthony Duclair Foundation’s mission is to ensure that BIPOC children are granted safe and equal access to playing hockey in the United States. As it stands right now, black, Indigenous, Hispanic and Latinos account for less than 15% of hockey players in the US. These children are severely under-represented.

Anthony’s Story

A child of Haitian immigrants, growing up as a black hockey player was never easy. Anthony’s difficult path has motivated him to knock down barriers and provide equal access and safe environments for people of colour to play hockey. Advocate for equality, philanthropist and athlete – there is more to Anthony than meets the eye.
